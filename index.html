About Me

I’m Dharineesh K S, a Developer and Designer with a B.Tech degree and expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Python. Transitioning from a career in banking, I bring a unique combination of analytical, problem-solving and creative design to the tech world. My time in the banking sector sharpened my attention to detail, adaptability, flexibility, and analytical Skills that I now leverage to develop high quality, responsive websites.

In my development journey, I’ve built and deployed a variety of projects that demonstrate my technical abilities and commitment to clean, user-friendly designs. Each project is a reflection of my ability to balance functionality with design, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and robust performance.

My educational background in B.Tech has provided me with a strong technical foundation and a problem-solving mindset, which I continually apply to my development work. This education, combined with my hands-on project experience, ensures that I approach every task with both a creative and methodical approach.

I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest web trends and best practices, and I’m eager to bring my technical skills, project experience, and strong work ethic to a dynamic tech team, where I can contribute to innovative projects and continue to grow as a professional.
